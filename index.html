Simple Cooking Recipes

1. Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk.
  2. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour in the eggs and stir gently until cooked.

2. Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides.
  3. Grill in a pan over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.

3. Banana Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Peel the banana and place in a blender.
  2. Add milk and honey.
  3. Blend until smooth and enjoy!
